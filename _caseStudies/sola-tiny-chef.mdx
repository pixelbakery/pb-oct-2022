<CaseStudiesIntro title={"Bringing a Tiny Chef with big, keto-friendly ideas to life."}>

The SOLA Company is a low-carb, low-sugar, keto-friendly brand that came to Pixel Bakery looking to make an [animated explainer video](/services/animated-explainer-videos) showcasing their bread.

We had previously worked with SOLA in the [live-action](/services/video-production) realm and were extremely excited to see what we could do for them within another specialty of ours - animation.
