<CaseStudiesIntro title={"Simplifying Complex Technical Concepts into a Digestible Video"}>
PB was tasked with creating a 120-second animated explainer video that introduces [Firework's](https://firework.com) video commerce SaaS technology and its a benefit to brands and retail. The video's objective was to simplify complex technical concepts into a digestible, compelling video that clearly lands what makes Firework unique.

**Industry:** [SaaS and Startups](/industries/saas-video-production)

**Key Messages**

**1:** To reveal the indispensable value of Firework. They are not a video player company. Firework designed all the important tech layers to ensure you a stable, secure, measurable, shoppable and interactive online experience, which is difficult to be achieved by in-house efforts alone.

**2:** Firework is confident that they can solve their customer's commerce problems with extreme speed since they are the leading in video commerce technology and they have a robust talent team to support any request their clients may have.
</CaseStudiesIntro>

<CaseStudiesDescription textColor={'wine'} bgColor={'egg'} headerColor={'blue-dark'} header={'Target Audience'}>
The target audience for this explainer was decision makers that work for potential customers. This audience tends to be be more informed about the backend of their own product. The potential customers are larger businesses that require enterprise-level security standards, and those who care deeply about optimization/site speed.

**General digital marketing/ eCommerce team:** to give them digestible info such as secure/ no affect on loading speed/ global streaming.

**Tech team:** to relieve their concerns about tech integration/ secure measurement and give them a better understanding of FW holistic tech capability.

</CaseStudiesDescription>
